# Transfers Detector

Anomalies examples:

* Large Transfers of ERC-20 or Native tokens
* Frequent Transfers


**Detector Configuration**  
1. *Name* - Enter a descriptive name for your monitor, for example: "Transfers".
2. *Token*
3. *Track From*
4. *Track To*
5. *From*
6. *To*
7. *Track Address*
8. *Track Frequent*
9. *Cron*
